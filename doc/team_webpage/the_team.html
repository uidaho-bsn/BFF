Jacob Flynn
Born in Boise, Jacob is a 23 year old senior in Computer Science with two semesters remaining. When programming he enjoys artificial intelligence the most, with anything that doesn't require any artistic talent in a close second. In his free time he plays/develops video games, hangs out with his fellow gamer friends, or a combination of the three. He works of the University of Idaho ITS Help Desk where he learns new ways to break computers all day. He is excited about creating the Bassoon App because it is a chance to create a real product that could actually be used, plus it is an opportunity to learn more about mobile devices and how they interpert web apps.
