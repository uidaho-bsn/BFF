Welcome to Team Bassoon App's webpage!

February 8, 2012Posted by Max

 

Have you ever wanted to know the best bassoon fingering to use for a passage of music but did not want to look through pages upon pages of fingerings? Well then, Bassoon Fingering Finder is for you! Bassoon Fingering Finder is a web application for looking up bassoon fingerings for a note or series of notes. Bassoon Fingering Finder is currently in development by a Senior Design Team from the University of Idaho and sponsored by Dr. Susan Hess. Questions or suggestions? Send us an email at bassoonfingeringfinder@gmail.com!
